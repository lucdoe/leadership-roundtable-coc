Code of Conduct

The Code of Conduct closely follows the values of the organisers and Netlight as a whole. To start off with, it is worth mentioning that the below applies both online and offline.

Zero tolerance for inappropriate behaviour

The Leadership Roundtable community has a zero-tolerance policy towards all forms of discrimination, harassment, degrading treatment and retaliation. To prevent and stop such behaviour, we advise all community members always to be mindful of everyone’s privacy and psychological safety. If you encounter any kind of inappropriate behaviour, be it towards yourself or a third party, please reach out to one of our drivers. We will always respect confidentiality requests to protect victims of abuse.

Encouragement and Mentorship (JC is this aligned with what the community is about?)

As the Leadership Roundtable community, we believe in the power of mentorship and positively inspiring each other. We are committed to fostering an environment where all members, regardless of their experience level, background, or identity, feel valued and supported to grow. Every community member is encouraged to seek mentoring opportunities as mentors or mentees and learn from each other's experiences and perspectives. Our community thrives when we share knowledge openly, support one another's growth and act as positive role models.

Advertising & Recruiting

We are more than happy for our community members to advertise their own events, recruit their fellow peers, or use the community in any other way for each other’s mutual benefit. However, we ask members to exercise a modicum of discretion and care in doing so. After all, we aim to cultivate our local communities around knowledge sharing and peer-to-peer support rather than an advertising platform. Therefore, the drivers reserve the right to direct such communication to specified Slack channels or remove unwanted content altogether.
